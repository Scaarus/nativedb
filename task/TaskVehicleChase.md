## TASK_VEHICLE_CHASE

```c
// 0x3C08A8E30363B353
void TASK_VEHICLE_CHASE(Ped ped, Ped ped);
```

The ped will chase the target ped's vehicle with their own vehicle.

Tells a ped to chase another ped. Both peds must be in vehicles, or the task will abort.
