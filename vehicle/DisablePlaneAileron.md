## DISABLE_PLANE_AILERON

```c
// 0x23428FC53C60919C
void DISABLE_PLANE_AILERON(Vehicle vehicle, bool LeftSide, bool Disable);
```

Disable movement of aileron and effect handling.

plane vehicle index, whether you want to effect the left or right hand aileron(facing from back of plane), enable or disable aileron control

Disable or re-enable the planes ailerons(large flaps on wings) Can be used to prevent movement and to effect the handling of a plane
